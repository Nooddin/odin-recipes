Kabab

Description

Kabab is love, kabab is life.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Oyl
  • Salt
  • Coriander
  • Black pepper
  • Ground pulses
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients.
  2. Make kabab using the mixture using seekhs.
  3. Fire up the barbecue.
  4. Grill the kababs on seekhs.
  5. Remove when cooked, let them cool.
  6. Eat.
