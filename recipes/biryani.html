Biryani

Description

Biryani is made with rice and meat. It's succulent, rich, and fulfilling.

It's a fragrant amalgamation of all the spices you can find in your cupboard. Let's see how to make the best biryani ever!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef
  • Onyo
  • Oyl
  • Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Take a large vessel.
  2. Add in oyl.
  3. Fry the sliced onyo.
  4. Add the beef and stir-fry it.
  5. Add the spices.
  6. Add water.
  7. Cover and cook until cooked.
  8. Remove the lid and add rice. Make sure that you have a nice and even layer of rice over the meat.
  9. Cover and let it cook for a couple of minutes.
  10. Eat.
